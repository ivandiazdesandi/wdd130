White Water Rafting Co.

We are passionate about adventure and nature. Our mission is to provide unforgettable rafting experiences for thrill-seekers of all kinds.

History

Founded in 1998, our company has guided thousands through thrilling rapids with safety and fun as our top priorities. From humble beginnings with just two rafts, we’ve grown to be a leader in white water adventures.

Our guides are certified, experienced, and passionate about the outdoors. Whether you’re new to rafting or a seasoned paddler, we have the perfect trip for you.
